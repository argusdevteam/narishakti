The Rollout - Conclave Sessions

Three sessions designed for dialogue, performance and celebration.

Session 1

Panel Discussion

An open debate on empowering women of Odisha, challenges and the way forward, with panelists from different professions.

Session 2

Creative Talent

A showcase of dance, song, music and creative performances to help participants bring out their latent talent.

Session 3

Mega Cooking Competition

A platform for women across Odisha to present traditional and fusion dishes, with auditions for the food show Swaad Odisha.
