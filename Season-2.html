Women Empowerment

When women prosper, the world prospers.

Women are the epitome of strength, love, sacrifice and courage. Yet many continue to shoulder greater responsibilities while struggling to stay alongside men in the workforce and public life.

According to the Global Gender Gap Report 2023, the world had closed 68.4% of the gender gap. Nari Shakti Season 2 carries the belief that empowerment can drive growth, social progress and a more inclusive society.

Strength

Honouring resilience, effort and everyday courage.

Opportunity

Opening a stage for talent, ideas and dreams.

Give wings to your talent and fulfil your dreams

Season 2 celebrates women who turn ambition, skill and responsibility into stories worth sharing.

The Intent

Empowering women builds stronger families, communities and nations.

Engage

Bring women together across residential societies, community centres and women's clubs.

Enable

Provide a meaningful platform where participants can share experiences, talent and practical skills.

Empower

Recognise and reward women for their achievements while encouraging them to pursue their passions.

Nari Shakti Unlimited 2023

Conducted in residential societies and community centres in Bhubaneswar, Angul, Puri, Sambalpur, Cuttack and Berhampur.

Activity 1

Panel Discussion

Women achievers of Odisha select themes of empowerment and share talks, experiences and reflections with the audience.

Activity 2

Stage Performance

Participants showcase talents such as dancing, singing, music, stand-up, comedy and painting before a jury panel.

Activity 3

Cooking Competition

Participants prepare and share recipes, judged by a jury, with winners receiving prizes and a chance to participate in the cooking show Swaad Odisha.

Season 2 Lookback

Moments from the stage, performances, audience and community events.

On-ground Reach

The season visited leading Odisha cities and created a space where women could participate, perform and be recognised in front of their communities.
